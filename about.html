I'm String Nguyen — 3× LinkedIn Top Voice, creator of the PACE Framework, and the person who'll tell you the truth about why your marketing isn't working. I help solopreneurs get found without becoming someone they're not.

The story

How I got here.

I didn't start out as a marketing person. I started out as someone who was good at their job and terrible at telling anyone about it. Back then, I thought if the work was good enough, people would just find you. They don't.

In 2016, I discovered live streaming — Periscope, Meerkat, whatever platform would let you talk directly to real people. Something clicked. Not because I suddenly loved being on camera, but because I realised being specific and being yourself was the whole game.

I went all in. I started posting on LinkedIn when most people were using it as a digital CV. I got it wrong, then less wrong, then started getting it right. Three times, LinkedIn named me a Top Voice. More importantly, I started getting clients — from my content, not from cold pitching.

So I built The Trusted Voice and created the PACE Framework — Position, Audience, Content, Engage — because solopreneurs kept asking me how I did it. Not a tips list. A system. One that works with AI, fits a real life, and doesn't require you to reinvent yourself.

Today I work with solopreneurs across Australia and globally — coaches, agency owners, consultants, experts who are brilliant at the work and invisible online. That gap between what you know and what the world sees? That's the gap I work in. 🍗

What I believe

The principles
I work by.

These aren't values from a brand workshop. They're the things I've said out loud in coaching sessions so many times they became doctrine.

  • Messy action beats perfect planning.Your first 10 posts will probably suck. Post them anyway. You don't get good by planning — you get good by publishing.
  • Clarity is the strategy.You don't have a content problem. You have a clarity problem. When you know exactly who you're talking to and what you stand for — showing up stops feeling hard.
  • You're not bad at marketing. You just haven't found your voice yet.Most solopreneurs who struggle online are trying to sound like someone else. The moment you sound like you — everything changes.
  • AI is a tool, not a replacement.AI can help you move faster, write more, show up more consistently. But it can't replace your story, your perspective, or your personality.
